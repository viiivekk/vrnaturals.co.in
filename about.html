About VR Naturals

Tagline: We Are Naturals.

Location: Pune, Maharashtra

Welcome to VR Naturals, where our identity is our promise — "We Are Naturals." This is not just a tagline; it is the philosophy that guides every aspect of our farm.

Based in the lush outskirts of Pune, we are dedicated to reviving the purest form of poultry farming. In a world moving toward artificial growth, we choose to slow down and let nature take the lead. We specialize in raising authentic Desi and Gawran poultry using traditional, organic methods.

Our Philosophy: We Are Naturals

  • Naturally Grown: Our birds are raised in a free-range, stress-free environment and grow at their natural pace, resulting in healthier meat and eggs.
  • Natural Diet: Our poultry feeds on a mix of natural grains like jowar, maize, and rice, along with foraging in open pastures.
  • Natural Ethics: We follow sustainable and clean farming practices that respect both nature and our livestock.

Nutritional Benefits of Desi Eggs

Eggs are one of nature’s most complete foods. Our naturally raised Desi eggs are packed with essential nutrients that support overall health.

  • High-quality protein for muscle growth and repair
  • Omega-3 fatty acids for heart and brain health
  • Vitamin B12 for energy and blood health
  • Vitamin D for strong bones
  • Choline for brain function
  • Rich in minerals like zinc, iron, and selenium
